Undergraduate Degree

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  1. Intermediate students who will be taking both intermediate part 1 & part 2 exams in June/July 2021
  2. Intermediate students whose intermediate part 1 & part 2 are clear.
  3. O Level students who have 8 O Levels cleared, with Mathematics and 3 Compulsory subjects including Pakistan Studies, Islamiyat and Urdu
  4. O Level students who will be taking 3 Principal subjects in May/June 2021
  5. Students who have completed their 8 O Levels and 3 A Level subjects (no AS grades accepted)
  6. Students who will be taking the Technical board exams in 2021
  7. Students who will be completing their International Baccalaureate in June / July 2021

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS FOR INTERMMEDIATE BOARD

  1. Attested copy of the matric result card
  2. Clear copy of the Bey form / CNIC card
  3. Clear copy of the Father / guardian CNIC card
  4. Passport size recent photograph with white background
  5. Paid receipt of the application processing fee of Rs.3500

FOR O/A LEVEL AND INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS

  1. Attested copies of 8 O level grades
  2. Attested copy of the O level equivalence issued by the IBCC
  3. Attested copies of A level grades if both O and A level is complete
  4. Attested copy of the O/|A level equivalence issued by the IBCC
  5. Copy of the statement of entry
  6. Clear copy of the Bey form / CNIC card
  7. Clear copy of the Father / guardian CNIC card
  8. Passport size recent photograph with white background
  9. Paid receipt of the application processing fee of Rs.3500
